About Us

WELCOME TO CLOVES

We don’t just make food. We make people’s experience
Chef Patron Joan Smith and Executive Chef François Lemoine have teamed up to open one of the most exciting restaurants in Anytown.
Our speciality is delicious cuisine created with the ingenuity and passion inspired by the fantastic flavours of France itself.
We take great pride in improving upon your favourite French dishes in new and creative ways. Cloves was built on the belief that food should be special, and we carry that belief into everything we do.

RESERVATIONS

Reservations open 30 days in advance.

We take reservations for dinner seven nights a week, 5:00–10:00.

We take reservations for lunch on Sundays from 12:00 until 3:00 p.m.

We also accept walk-ins based on availability.

For parties of 7 or more guests and special events, please click here.

Delicious Bites is a very cosy space. If you are more than 15 minutes late for your reservation, your table will be given away to a waiting guest.

We are currently accepting reservations for three different areas:

Indoors – Our dining room and front bar room

Cabin – Enclosed, communal outdoor dining with heating

Terrace – Open-air outdoor dining on the sidewalk with minimal heating

Cancellation Policy: Should you cancel within 12 hours of your reservation or no show, you will be held to a $50 per person late cancellation fee.
Please note, we only accept cancellations directly through the Resy app.
